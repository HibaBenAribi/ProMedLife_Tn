Story n°1
Healthy
Village
In a village renowned for its healthy lifestyle, a magical candy store tempts children away from nutritious fruits, leading to a decline in health until a group of concerned mothers and a wise doctor rally to restore the village's vitality through wholesome foods and education on the benefits of natural sugars.
Story n°2
The Fruitful
Adventure
A lively primary school class learns about healthy snacks when their friend Timmy falls ill from eating too many unhealthy treats. With the guidance of their teacher, Mrs. Parker, they discover the magic of fruits as natural, delicious, and nourishing alternatives that protect their bodies like superheroes and bring joy to snack time.
Story n°3
The Magical
Dates
Sara and Adam embark on a magical journey with their wise grandfather Baba Ahmed, learning about the diverse varieties of dates and discovering the hidden superpowers of these delicious fruits packed with polyphenols.
Story n°4
Sweet Surprises
with Dates
Lily and Ben discover the sweet and healthy magic of dates as their grandmother introduces them to using dates as a natural sweetener in delicious treats, making their visits to Grandma and Grandpa's house filled with sweet surprises and healthy delights.
Story n°4
Oliver the Onion:
A Sweet Surprise for Veggieland
Mrs. Thompson transforms the children's perception of onions by introducing them to tasty and nutritious onion snacks, helping them appreciate onions as valuable protectors and boosting their culinary creativity in Veggieland.
